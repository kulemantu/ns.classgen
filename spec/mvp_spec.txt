THE PROBLEM 
Teachers want to make lessons more engaging, but they lack the time and creative support to constantly design interactive 
activities. Most fall back on lecturing — not because they want to, but because preparation takes 30–60 minutes they don't 
have. 
THE SOLUTION 
ClassGen is a lightweight AI tool that converts a lesson topic into a ready-to-use classroom activity pack in under one minute. 
A teacher enters basic class details and receives a structured lesson agenda they can use that same day — no design work, 
no blank-page anxiety. 
V1 INPUT → OUTPUT 
TEACHER ENTERS (Input) 
CLASSGEN RETURNS (Output) 
• Country (e.g. Nigeria) 
• Class level (e.g. SS3 / Form 4) 
• Subject (e.g. Mathematics) 
• Topic / Chapter (e.g. Calculus) 
• Duration (e.g. 40 minutes) 
Plan A  —  structured & teacher-led 
Plan B  —  group-based & participatory 
 
Each plan includes: 
  1. Concept explanation (plain language) 
  2. Real-world relevance / hook 
  3. Lesson opener / icebreaker 
  4. Main activity or group challenge 
  5. Homework (scenario-based) 
  6. Teacher notes & expected answers 
 
The teacher reviews both plans and uses whichever fits their class. No login required for the initial prototype.

HOW WE VALIDATE 
• 
① Generate 3 sample lesson packs manually (Maths, English, Science). 
• 
② Show them to 5 secondary school teachers — ask: "Would you use this tomorrow morning?" 
• 
③ Build narrowest WhatsApp prototype; measure: repeat use within 7 days. 
Success signal: at least 3 of 5 teachers say they'd use Plan A or Plan B without editing. 
WHY NOT JUST CHATGPT? 
• 
Structured, teacher-ready format — not a raw AI chat response 
• 
Two curated delivery options per lesson — instant choice, zero effort 
• 
Age-appropriate, curriculum-aware tone and activity design
