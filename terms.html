What ClassGen Does

ClassGen is an AI-powered lesson plan generator for secondary school teachers. You send a topic, and it generates a structured lesson pack with teaching materials, activities, and homework assignments.

ClassGen is a teaching aid. Lesson content is AI-generated and should be reviewed by the teacher before classroom use. We do not guarantee the accuracy of generated content.

What We Collect

  • Messages you send — your topic requests and conversation history are stored to provide context for lesson generation.
  • Phone number (WhatsApp users) — used as your account identifier. Not shared with third parties.
  • Student quiz submissions — student name, class, and quiz answers are stored to provide results to teachers.
  • Teacher profile — name, school, and classes you add. Used for your public profile page.

How We Use Your Data

  • To generate and deliver lesson packs
  • To provide homework quiz functionality for your students
  • To display your teacher profile and lesson statistics
  • To improve the quality of generated content

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties beyond the AI service provider (OpenRouter) that generates lesson content.

Data Retention

  • Homework codes expire after 14 days
  • Generated PDFs are cleaned up within 24 hours
  • Conversation history is kept to provide context for future lessons
  • You can clear your chat history at any time from the Settings panel

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Teachers can clear their own chat history and remove their profile from the Settings panel.

Third-Party Services

  • OpenRouter — AI model provider for lesson generation
  • Twilio — WhatsApp message delivery (WhatsApp users only)
